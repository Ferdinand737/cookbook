Spicy Egg Pan

500 g leek
1 onion
125 g fresh mushrooms
2 tomatoes
75 g breakfast bacon
4 eggs
4 tbsp canned milk
Salt, pepper, paprika
80 g Gouda or Emmental cheese

Clean and slice leek. Dice onion and mushrooms. Cut tomatoes into pieces. Fry bacon until crispy, remove and set aside. Saut√© onion in bacon fat, add leek and mushrooms, cook until tender. Add tomatoes and cook briefly. Beat eggs with milk, season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Pour egg mixture over vegetables, add bacon back. Cook until eggs are set. Sprinkle with grated cheese and let melt.
