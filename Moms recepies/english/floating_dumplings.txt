Floating Dumplings

100 ml milk
50 g butter
60 g flour
2 eggs
Nutmeg, pepper, salt

Put milk and butter in pot and bring to boil. Add flour and stir until a flour ball forms. Put this in bowl and immediately stir in 1 egg. Only add the 2nd egg when a smooth dough has formed again. Work in well too. Season mixture with nutmeg, pepper and salt, then use small spoons to drop dumplings into hot soup and simmer for 5-8 minutes at lowest heat setting.
