Savoy Cabbage Casserole

1 kg savoy cabbage
100 g rice
500 g ground meat
Salt, pepper
60 g fat
Sour cream

Cut savoy cabbage into wide strips, cook in salt water until tender, drain and let drip. Saut√© diced onion in fat until translucent, add ground meat and cook until meat turns gray. Mix with rice. Grease form with fat, layer savoy cabbage and ground meat with rice alternately. Top layer should be savoy cabbage. Brush with melted fat and sour cream and bake golden brown in oven.
