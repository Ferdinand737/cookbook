Serbian Rice Meat (Cooking - The Young School)

500 g pork
Oil
3 onions
1 garlic clove
40 g tomato paste
3/4 l water
1 broth cube
2 tsp dried thyme
Paprika powder
150 g long grain rice
2 bell peppers
1 fresh chili pepper

Cut pork into pieces and brown in oil. Add diced onions and garlic, saut√© until translucent. Stir in tomato paste, add water and broth cube. Season with thyme and paprika. Add rice and diced bell peppers. Add chili pepper. Simmer until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 20-25 minutes.
