Swallows' Nests

4 thin schnitzels
4 slices Gouda
4 slices cooked ham
Salt, pepper
1 tbsp tomato paste
4 hard-boiled eggs
2 raw eggs
Breadcrumbs
2 tbsp Emmental
Fat for frying

Spread schnitzels thinly with tomato paste, sprinkle with salt and pepper, top with a slice of ham. Add some tomato paste on top, then a slice of cheese. Place a hard-boiled egg on each schnitzel, tie schnitzels well into a roulade over the egg. Turn roulades in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs mixed with grated cheese. Deep fry in hot fat until golden.
