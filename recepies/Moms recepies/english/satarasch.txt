Satarasch (2 Persons)

200-250 g pork schnitzel
3 large onions
2 bell peppers
Oil, tomato paste, 1 garlic clove
4 tbsp cream
4 tbsp broth or water
Salt, paprika, pepper etc.

Put schnitzel in freezer for 15-30 min, then cut into narrow strips. Peel onions and cut into slices. Wash bell peppers and cut into strips. Fry vegetables, add meat, fry for 3 min. Add tomato paste, garlic, cream and broth, bring to simmer. Season with salt, pepper and paprika.
