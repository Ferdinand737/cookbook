Zurich Veal Roulades

4 large, thin veal schnitzels
Salt, paprika
4 slices lean, cooked or raw ham
4 tbsp onions
Chopped parsley
10 g butter
4 hard-boiled eggs

Pound schnitzels, salt, sprinkle with paprika, fill. Top each schnitzel with a slice of ham, spread with saut√©ed onion and parsley, top with hard-boiled, peeled egg (possibly halved lengthwise), roll up, tie securely. Cook like beef roulades.
