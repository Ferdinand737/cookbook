Ground Meat Pâté

500g mixed ground meat
30g clarified butter
150g brown mushrooms
2 onions
1 red bell pepper
3 eggs
1 pot coriander
Salt, pepper
2 tbsp chili sauce
1 package frozen puff pastry
Flour
Breadcrumbs
2 tbsp milk
Fat for the baking sheet

Brown ground meat in clarified butter. Add diced onions, mushrooms, and bell pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender. Beat in eggs, season with coriander, salt, pepper, and chili sauce. Roll out puff pastry, place meat mixture in center. Fold pastry over filling and seal edges. Brush with milk and sprinkle with breadcrumbs. Bake on greased baking sheet according to pastry package instructions.
