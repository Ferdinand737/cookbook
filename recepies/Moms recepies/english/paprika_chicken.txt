Paprika Chicken

1 chicken
1 onion
Fat
Paprika
1 tomato
Salt, flour
Sour cream

Cut chicken into 8 pieces. Brown chopped onion in fat, add paprika, chicken pieces, quartered tomato, salt and 2 tbsp water. Braise chicken slowly with lid closed until tender. Mix flour with 2 tbsp sour cream, pour over chicken, bring to boil once. Serve with rice.
