Goulash Soup

500 g goulash meat
1 onion
1 garlic clove
1 bell pepper
2 tomatoes
500 g potatoes
2-3 carrots
1 slice celery
Salt, 1/2 tsp caraway seeds
2 tsp paprika

Cut goulash meat into pieces and brown. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until translucent. Add diced bell pepper, tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, and celery. Season with salt, caraway seeds, and paprika. Add enough water to cover ingredients. Simmer until meat is tender and vegetables are cooked through, about 1-1.5 hours.
