Mushroom Schnitzel (2 Persons)

2 schnitzels
Lemon juice
125-150 g fresh mushrooms
Oil, salt, flour
1/8 l cream
2 tbsp white wine

Pound schnitzels, sprinkle with lemon juice. Clean and wash mushrooms, cut into slices, sprinkle with lemon juice. Fry mushrooms, remove from pan. Sear meat well, continue frying 6-10 min, salt. Sprinkle flour over, deglaze with cream and wine. Add mushrooms and adjust seasoning.
