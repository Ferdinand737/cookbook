Quiche Lorraine

125g flour
80g butter
1 egg
1 tsp baking powder
150g cooked ham
250g grated cheese
2 egg yolks
125ml sour cream
Salt, paprika
Fat for the dish

Quickly combine flour, salt, egg, butter, and baking powder to form a smooth shortcrust pastry. Let it rest in the refrigerator for 1 hour. On a floured surface, roll out thinly and place into a greased dish. Cover with strips of ham, pour over the cheese mixture, and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 25 minutes. Serve immediately, cut into pieces.

Cheese mixture: Whisk together grated cheese, egg yolks, and sour cream, and season with paprika.
