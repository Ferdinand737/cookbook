Vegetable Soup with Sausages

200 g onions
1 stalk leek
200 g yellow turnips
400 g potatoes
30 g butter or margarine
1 l broth
Salt, pepper
100 g heavy cream
200 g sausages
1 bunch chives

Sauté onion cubes, leek rings, yellow turnips and potato cubes in hot fat, add broth, season, cook for about 15 min. Purée half the soup with immersion blender. Add to vegetables, pour in cream and bring to boil, adjust seasoning. Heat sausages in soup, serve sprinkled with chives.
