Colorful Onion Pot

500 g onions
2 tbsp long grain rice
1 tbsp instant broth
500 g tomatoes
500 g bell peppers
1 can corned beef (340g) or other meat
1 tsp paprika
½ bunch parsley
Oil for frying

Slice onions and sauté in oil until translucent. Add rice and instant broth. Add diced tomatoes and bell peppers. Stir in corned beef or other meat. Season with paprika. Simmer until rice is tender and vegetables are cooked through. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
