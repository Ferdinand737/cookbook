Tortellini

Option 1:

650g tortellini
20g butter
100g fresh mushrooms
100g cooked ham
500ml sweet cream
150g Parmesan

Cook tortellini in salted water or broth for 15 minutes. Melt butter, dice mushrooms and ham, and saut√©. Add cream and approximately 100g of Parmesan, stirring well. Simmer for 3-4 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Drain tortellini and let them sit in the cream sauce before serving, then sprinkle with the remaining cheese.

Option 2:

650g tortellini
20g butter
500ml sweet cream
250g peeled tomatoes
100g grated Parmesan

Cook tortellini in salted water or broth for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, melt butter in a pan, add peeled tomatoes, crush with a fork, and simmer with the lid on for 5 minutes. Drain the cooked tortellini and add them to the pan with the sauce. Stir in the sweet cream and season with salt and pepper. Stir well, serve, and sprinkle with Parmesan.
