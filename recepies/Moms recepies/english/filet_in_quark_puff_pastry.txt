Filet in Quark Puff Pastry

1 kg pork filet
Quark Puff Pastry:            Filling:
200 g flour                   200 g sausage meat
200 g butter                  2 eggs
200 g quark                   1 cup crème fraîche
                              50 g chervil
                              1 small onion
                              Parsley
                              Salt, pepper, nutmeg

Sear meat well, spread filling on top, roll out dough, wrap meat in dough. Bake for 35 min.
